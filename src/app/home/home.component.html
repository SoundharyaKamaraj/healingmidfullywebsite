Ayla Rapoport, M.S., is a wellness consultant in the Boston area who has a background in meditation and arts creation. She has contributed to the wellbeing of medical students, patients in hospitals, and clinical staff through teaching and practicing the whole-personal approach to wellbeing. She has created an art-based healing project for Ronald McDonald House Charities affiliated with Cincinnati Children’s Hospital Medical Center, designed educational programming in emotional/spiritual aspects of care in The Christ Hospital, Cincinnati, and created an educational booklet and lecture for clinicians in emotional spiritual aspects of coping for the Division of Human Genetics at Cincinnati Children’s Hospital Medical Center.She is a member of the Osher Center for Integrative Medicine, a network through Brigham and Women’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School, SheBreathes Balance and Wellness Studio, and other innovative healthcare networks in Boston. Her meditation communities include the Cambridge Zen Center, Open Meadow Zen, and Boston Old Path Sangha.
